Billionaires in United States

In 2022, United States is the country with the highest number of billionaires worldwide.

Number of Billionaires in each state

This map visualisation indicates the number of billionaires per 100 million population in each state of the United States. More than 50% of the state owns less than 400 billionaires per 100 million population. The state with the highest number of billionaires per 100 million population is Wyoming with 1022 billionaires per 100 million population. The state without billionaires will be greyed out.

After hovering, double click on any empty space in the visualisation to view the overall colour opacity.

Billionaire's Gender

Each icon in this visualisation represents 30 billionaires. The difference between the number of male billionaires and female billionaires is huge. There are 22 icons representing 660 male billionaires and 3 icons representing 90 female billionaires. Thus, 88% of the billionaires in the United States are male billionaires.

Billionaire's Age Group

There are 3 age groups of billionaires such as Young Adult (Age Range 21-40), Middle Aged Adult (Age Range 41-60) and Old (Age Range 61 and above). The sequence of highest to lowest number of billionaires in each age group for both genders male and female are symmetrical.

65% of the billionaires are from old age. Followed by billionaires from middle age. Lastly, only 6% of billionaires are from a young age.

Billionaire's Net Worth

80% of the billionaires in each age group own less than 20 net worth per billion for both gender male and female.

The richest male billionaires falls in the middle age group which is Elon Musk who is 50 years old with 219 net worth per billion.

The richest female billionaires falls in the old age group which is Alice Walton who is 72 years old with 65.3 net worth per billion. The net worth per billion between male and female is huge too.

Billionaire's Self Made

80% of the male billionaires are self-made billionaires while only 26% of the female billionaires are self-made billionaires. Those self-made billionaires have become rich and successful through their own efforts.

Billionaire's Ranking

The Top 1 billionaire in the United States is Elon Musk while Alice Walton is the Top 12 billionaire in the United states. The average net worth per billion of the Top 10 billionaires in the United States is 120.

Billionaire's Company

At the first glance of the word cloud, the biggest font size of word is Investments, followed by Real Estate. These are the Top 2 companies where the majority of the billionaires are from.

Billionaire's Title based on Work Industry

Majority of the billionaires in each work industry is Chief Executive Officer, CEO. Sports industry is the only industry with an Athlete title as the title is only relevant to the respective industry.
